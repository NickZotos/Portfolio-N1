About Me

Let me introduce myself

During my formative years in high school at the Academy for Allied Health Sciences I had the opportunity to engage in diverse academic pursuits that kindled my passion for medicine. I actively participated in competitive events, such as interactive anatomical design, and took on a leadership role by guiding my school's debate team to victory in the championship. These experiences not only reinforced my enthusiasm for medical knowledge but also highlighted my keen interest in effective communication. Since then, I made a decision to contribute to the medical community and pursue a degree in biology at Duquesne Honors College. Beyond my formal studies, I have dedicated my free time to cultivating personal projects, with one notable pursuit being science journalism. To enhance my skills beyond the confines of the classroom, I actively participate in my college's newspaper and write as a senior writer for the D.U Quark, our campus's science journal. Looking ahead, I remain eager to explore various facets of the medical field, seeking to expand my expertise and gain experiences in diverse sectors of medicine. My aspiration is to contribute significantly to medical communication advancements. If you wish to delve deeper into my background and accomplishments, please see my CV provided below.
